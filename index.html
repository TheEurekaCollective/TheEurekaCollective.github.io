S Y N O P S I S

It is the year 2570. Humanity has discovered magic, blending it with technology to bring about a golden age. The line between man and machine has been blurred, with cybernetics and genetic modification becoming the norm. By compressing space, the speed of light has been surpassed; allowing humanity to take to far-off stars. However, those stars were not empty.

A thousand galaxy-spanning civilizations have lived and died before the turning of humanityâ€™s first wheel. These civilizations have left behind relics of both boons and banes. Religious robots, fleets of suns, and gods born of belief and prayer have been rendered commonplace. Humanity has been introduced to a galaxy full of danger, mystery, but most importantly, adventure.
