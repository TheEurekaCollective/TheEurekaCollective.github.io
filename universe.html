PROJECT ANIMA
Lore Art Universe About Us

Overview

Project Anima is a science-fantasy universe set in a future of science and magic. It takes place in an alternate timeline where the branch of paraphysics known as thaumaturgy had been rediscovered by scientists during the mid-twentieth century. Project Anima follows humanity as it grows and explores the Milky Way Galaxy.

Origin

Ever since I was a young child, science fiction and fantasy universes had always captivated me. I loved the SCP Foundation for its mystique and horror, Warhammer 40k for its grimdark setting and sheer brutality, and the Destiny universe for its mixture of magic and technology. These were just some of the worlds that I spent my time delving into. When the coronavirus pandemic struck, I was forced to spend a large amount of time indoors with nothing to do. That was when I had one thought: “Magic and guns”. It was a narrow thought, but it eventually served as the seed for the universe would become Project Anima.

Sandbox

MOOD

The mood for Project Anima is True Neutral. This means that in this universe, while there are a lot of black and white aspects, the majority is gray. The inhabitants of the Milky Way are able to influence the events for better or worse.

GENRES

Project Anima is host to genres such as science-fantasy, historical fiction, action, adventure, futurism, magipunk, solarpunk, and postcyberpunk.

THEMES

Project Anima explores themes such as transhumanism, free will, social engineering, xeno-relativism, religion, immortality, and identity.

SCALE

While there are happenings in other galaxies, the majority of Project Anima’s events and information stems from the Milky Way. In terms of time, the events of Project Anima Began nearly one billion years ago.

CHRONOLOGY

The POD (point-of-divergence) for humanity is sometime during the mid-twentieth century. This means that events that occurred in the real world were either changed or didn’t occur entirely.

UNIVERSAL LAWS

On top of paraphysics, several additions to normal physics have been added. This includes the introduction of the graviton and wormholes. Alternate timelines and time travel have been prohibited.
