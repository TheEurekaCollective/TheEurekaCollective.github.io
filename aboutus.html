About

The Eureka Collective is a group of worldbuilders dedicated to bringing worlds such as Project Anima to life. While the idea was initially conceptualized in the summer of 2020, we were founded in the spring of 2021 and our operations began in the summer of 2021. While currently consisting of only ten members, we hope to expand this organization further to not only increase the content of existing worlds but also create new worlds in the future.
